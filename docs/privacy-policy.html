Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

RanJitzu does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app works entirely on your device. We believe training tools should be simple and private.

Who We Are

RanJitzu is developed and maintained by Albert Labs, an independent software developer. You can contact us at a.ovodas@gmail.com.

Data We Collect

We collect no personal data whatsoever. Specifically:

  • No account creation or login required
  • No name, email, or contact information collected
  • No location data collected
  • No device identifiers collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers used

Data Stored on Your Device

RanJitzu stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Match history (fighter names, scores, prompts, timestamps)
  • This data never leaves your device
  • It is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • You can clear it at any time from the History screen

Feedback Submissions

If you choose to send feedback through the in-app feedback form, your message is sent to our feedback service (Formspree). This submission contains only the text you write — no device information, no personal data, and no identifying information is attached automatically.

Submitting feedback is entirely optional and voluntary.

Third-Party Services

  • Formspree — used only for optional feedback submissions. View their privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy
  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics platforms
  • No social media SDKs

Children's Privacy

RanJitzu does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
